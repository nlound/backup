This week, learn how to create The Illusion Effect with your Galaxy Note9.
Add magic to your coffee photo
Go outside with a friend and a coffee cup. Switch your camera to Auto Mode. Take a Burst Shot as they throw the cup. Just press and hold the shoot button. Find the image where the cup is at its peak.

Change up your mirror selfie
Place a travel mirror in the grass. Angle your phone at it and set it down. Now stand in the reflected spot. Strike a pose. And click your S Pen to take the shot.
