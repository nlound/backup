Take your photography to the next level with photo tips.
This week, learn how to create The Refraction Effect with your Galaxy Note9.
Shake things up
Mix a drink and take it to a sunny spot. The more detailed the glass, the better. Tilt your glass to cast an intricate shadow. On Pro Mode, set the aperture to f2.4. Then drag the shutter speed and shoot.
Give your photos a twist
Play around with the composition. Add glasses and objects to the setting. Arrange them to create harsh shadows.
Shoot old favorites in new ways
Add some more visual interest. Get your hand in the photo. Tap your glass to focus on it. Then take the shot.
