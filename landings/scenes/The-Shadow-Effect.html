Take your photography to the next level with photo tips.
This week, learn how to create The Shadow Effect with your Galaxy Note9.
See portraits in a new light
Go in a room near a streetlight at night. Position a friend in front of the blinds. Open and close them so shadows appear. On Pro Mode, set your aperture to f1.5. Drag the shutter speed to darken your shot. Then take the photo.
Shoot your shadow
Find some bleachers on a sunny day. Set your Galaxy Note9 on a stable surface. Stand so your shadow is cast onto the steps. Play with a few different poses. Then click your S Pen to take the picture.
Be the canvas
Step into a sunny room with lace curtains. Position a friend in front of the sun. Move the curtains so a light tattoo forms. Tap the brightest part of their skin. Then take the shot.
