The Magic
Hour Effect
Take your photography to the next level with photo tips.
This week, learn how to create The Magic Hour Effect with your Galaxy Note9.
Add some magic
Go to your favorite spot at magic hour. Set your camera on a tripod or surface. Tap the sun once it hits the trees. Then take your photo.
Selfie differently
Place your partner in front of a sunset. Give them a blanket to hold over their body. Place your camera on a tripod or surface. Switch your Galaxy Note9 to Pro Mode. Then, set the aperture to f2.4. Join them and take the shot with your S Pen.
Make magic with your camera
Sit facing a landscape at magic hour. The first and last hour of sunlight each day. Stretch out your legs as far as you can. Switch your Galaxy Note9 to Auto Mode. Wait for the sunset icon to appear. Then tap your feet and take your shot.
