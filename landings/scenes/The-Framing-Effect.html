Take your photography to the next level with photo tips.
This week, learn how to create The Framing Effect with your Galaxy Note9.
Put the frame in your photo
Place your Galaxy Note9 on a stable surface. Point it at a window with a view. Drag the brightness to adjust the lighting. Lock the focus on the frame and take a seat. Then click your S Pen to take the photo.
Round is the new square
Position a friend in front of a hollow object. Line up a shot on the other side. Lock the focus on your subject. Just hold your finger on the screen. Then take the shot.
Frame up a new scene
Turn an object or structure into a frame. First, position your subject in the middle. Switch your camera to Auto Mode. Hold your thumb on your subject. Then take your shot.
