What is this?

In the not-so-distant past, Digital Advertising meant one thing: banners. But in reality, Digital Advertising is simply advertising that happens on a screen.

Because this advertising is connected between screens, it needs to be connected to a campaign system as a whole.

Campaign systems are necessarily flexible: depending on the budget, goals, pre-determined media, and available data, no two are the same. But what does not change is the fact that every consumer touch —every message, every ad — is connected to its context, its available data and its viewer.

Ultimately what makes Digital Advertising unique is its ability to measure direct response through a host of metrics, its ability to connect all of these screens to one main repository of content and business rules, its ability to target users through context and behavior, and its ability to scale inexpensively.

Where does it fit within R/GA?

The Digital Advertising group is a bit of an anomaly at R/GA, because we have a flexible client-group model.

The core of what we offer to all clients is Strategy, Creative (design/copy), Production, and Technology.

For many clients we provide a “complete package,” meaning that we have client services, strategy, research, production, creative, technology and analytics housed within the group.

For others we flex to include only the needed components, e.g. Production and Technology support with Creative and Strategic consultation.

When do you need it?

As soon as you think you know a campaign is coming. Seriously.

Many campaigns come and go on the merits of our initial conversations. If we don’t provide a strong leadership position, as opposed to a production-oriented position, we will lose campaigns to low-cost (and low-quality) production houses. Our platforms will suffer, and the clients’ traffic will suffer too.

Our team can help sort out a confusing external media plan, help ideate with new technology, and can provide years of best-practices to help size the response to the ask. And we’re told we’re really pleasant to work with.

For most client projects we are brought in at the briefing stage, to help draft messaging and media strategies (in conjunction with the media and CRM groups (be they R/GA media or another agency). Our producers work with the client team producers to craft production schedules and costs. We develop creative concepts in conjunction with the Group Creative or Executive Sponsor as appropriate. We work with client services to help sell sometimes-complex technical executions. And, of course, we build the ads.

How we do it?

Efficient cooperative workflow

We have a customized internal production process that allows us to quickly create display ads that will not only be optimized to reduce file size but will also run across all current desktop and mobile environments. Our workflow simultaneously incorporates a code sharing methodology, with the expectation that at all of our developers have a common code base to easily share and update projects.

We can accomplish this by leveraging our wide scope of technical proficiency in:

  • HTML5/CSS/JavaScript/Sass/Node
  • Task Runners like Gulp, Grunt and Codekit
  • Git
  • Image and video optimization
